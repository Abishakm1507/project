Mood Tracking:

Users can select their current mood using an emoji-based interface. This feature promotes self-awareness and helps track emotional trends over time.

Daily Wellness Plans:

A structured timeline displays daily tasks like meditation, journaling, exercise, and gratitude reflection. Each task has a clear description and duration to guide users in achieving their wellness goals.

Habit Streaks:

The app motivates users by tracking their day streaks, providing a sense of accomplishment for maintaining consistent habits.

Challenges and Rewards:

Includes fun and engaging challenges like step quests, where users can achieve goals (e.g., 1,000 steps) to earn rewards like coins.

Daily Quiz:

Offers a short daily quiz to reinforce positive mental health habits and keep users engaged in learning.

Digital Detox:

Encourages users to step away from screens for a set duration to recharge mentally.

Interactive Design:

Tasks and challenges are interactive, with completion status visually updated, making the experience user-friendly and engaging.

Upcoming Challenges and Plans:

Keeps users informed about upcoming wellness activities and challenges, ensuring they stay on track with their mental health journey.
